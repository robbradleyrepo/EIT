Scam emails and websites

Occasionally websites appear that pretend to be Liontrust but which do not belong to us and are trying to get hold of your money fraudulently. There have also been recent instances of investors receiving emails from people impersonating Liontrust. We report these websites and emails to the appropriate authorities as soon as we become aware of them. Please beware of this illegal activity and look for liontrust.co.uk in the website name and email address. If you are suspicious of any communication purportedly from Liontrust, please contact us to confirm the website or email is legitimate by emailing us at info@liontrust.co.uk or calling 020 7412 1700.
