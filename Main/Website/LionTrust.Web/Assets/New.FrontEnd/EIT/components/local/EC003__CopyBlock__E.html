Why invest with us?

  • Our goal is to grow your capital and dividend payments regardless of economic and market conditions through a flexible investment process that is thoughtful and responsible.
  • The Trust currently has a dividend yield of 4.2% and a dividend reserve of 1.2 years. The latter means we can maintain the dividend for just over a year without any of the companies we invest in making payments.
  • We invest in growth and value companies as well as those able to recover through management change, business transformation and an improving business environment.
